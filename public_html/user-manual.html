Instructions

Credits and Player

  • Insert Coin / Press 'c' to recieve credits to play.
  • One credit will allow 1 player game mode to begin.
  • Two or more credits will allow 1 or 2 player game mode to begin.
  • Each credit will allow a maximum of 3 lives per player.
  • Once the appropriate credtis are entered choose 1 or 2 player mode by pressing '1' or '2'.

Objectives

  • Players control Pac-Man's movements and should avoid the 4 ghosts.
  • Pac-Man may move Up, Right, Down, and Left by using the arrow keys on the keyboard.
  • Pac-Man should try to eat all of the dots Each dot is worth 10 points.
  • There are also 4 flashing energizers that are worth 50 points.
  • After eating a flashing energizer Pac-Man can attack the ghosts that are blue.
  • Beware of flashing ghosts which are about to change back to dangerous colors.
  • Players may not move through walls or into ghost starting location.
  • Players and ghosts may teleport to the opposite side of the level by using the openings on the edges.
